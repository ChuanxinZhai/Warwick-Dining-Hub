About Us

Warwick Dining Hub offers a diverse range of dining facilities across the University of Warwick campus. From cozy cafes to elegant restaurants and lively bars, our hubs cater to every taste and occasion. Our mission is to provide delicious, high-quality food and drink options in a welcoming environment, making campus life more enjoyable for students, staff, and visitors. Join us to explore the culinary delights our dining facilities have to offer.

Provide Good Food and Drinks

We aim to provide delicious food and drinks to students and faculty so that they can enjoy their campus life to the fullest. All products are made from specially selected raw materials and are very healthy.

Staff Training

All our staff are paid the living wage and trained to the highest standards, with ongoing health, safety and hygiene training. We want to give you a great dining experience.

Sustainable Development

We use as much recyclable tableware as possible to minimize the production of pollutants. It is everyone's responsibility to protect the environment.
